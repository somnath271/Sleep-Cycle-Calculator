Why Sleep Matters

Health Benefits

Quality sleep is essential for maintaining good health and well-being. It helps:

  • Improve memory and cognitive function
  • Boost immune system
  • Regulate mood and reduce stress
  • Support physical recovery and growth

Sleep Cycle Science

Understanding your sleep cycles is crucial because:

  • Each cycle lasts about 90 minutes
  • Complete cycles are essential for restful sleep
  • Waking up between cycles leads to better alertness
  • Proper timing can improve sleep quality

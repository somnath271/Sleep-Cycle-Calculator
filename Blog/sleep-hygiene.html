What is Sleep Hygiene?

Sleep hygiene refers to the habits and practices that are conducive to sleeping well on a regular basis. It's about creating the perfect environment and routine for quality sleep.

Creating a Sleep-Friendly Environment

  • Keep your bedroom cool, dark, and quiet
  • Invest in a comfortable mattress and pillows
  • Use blackout curtains or an eye mask
  • Consider white noise machines or earplugs

Daily Habits for Better Sleep

Morning Routine

  • Wake up at the same time every day
  • Get exposure to natural light
  • Exercise regularly

Evening Routine

  • Limit screen time before bed
  • Avoid caffeine and heavy meals
  • Create a relaxing bedtime ritual

Common Sleep Hygiene Mistakes

Avoid these common pitfalls that can disrupt your sleep:
  • Using electronic devices in bed
  • Irregular sleep schedule
  • Consuming stimulants late in the day
  • Taking long naps during the day

Quick Tips

Remember that good sleep hygiene is a combination of consistent habits and a sleep-friendly environment. Start implementing these changes gradually and track your progress.
